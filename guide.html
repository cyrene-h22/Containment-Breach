Site Director's Notes

In this game, you have an "employee" score that is updated with each choice you make, and if you reach the game's real endings, the ending you get is affected by your game score. For example: choosing the option where you act professional in an interview makes you gain points and doing the opposite makes you lose points. There are six endings in total.

If you'd like to get the best ending, you must do the following: For the first task, be respectful towards your interviewees. For the second task, make sure to run when prompted. For the third task, don't look. You can do the opposite for the fired ending.
